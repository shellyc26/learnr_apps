2. Scale the SAT scores so that they are on the same scale as the ACT scores.

A perfect SAT score is 1600 and a perfect ACT score is 36. So let’s use a scaling factor of .0225 (.0025 x 1600 = 36). Call this new variable scaledSat and print it out.

scaledSat <- satTotal * .0225
